Educational Technology Manager


Are you interested in using your ed tech skills in a radically ambitious math education startup? If yes, read on.

Bravo Math is trying to create the best damn math course ever. Your role as the Educational Technology Manager will be to establish a learning management system (such as Canvas or Moodle) and an online math assessment system (such as iMathAS or WebWork). If that goes well, your duties will expand to include creating digital learning resources and activities and conducting informal user tests of them. If/When the business grows, you will manage a formal user testing lab and be offered broader duties generally, with no cap on compensation.

Educational Assistant

As a Bravo Math Educational Assistant, you will work part-time - maybe 3-5 hours/week - to help build Counting to Calculus, i.e. The Best Damn Math Course Ever. Your initial contributions will include building answer keys, creating different versions of exercises and activities, marking, and administrative work. After proving you can do those successfully, duties and compensation could expand to include researching and testing activities, creating them from scratch, evaluating them, assisting with tutoring, etc. Ultimately, I want your help in building resources for about 80 lessons so amazing that they transform any student's life and transform YOU into an amazing educator. Along the way, you will learn a ton about education, psychology, math, and you might just become an amazing math tutor and business owner as well.
