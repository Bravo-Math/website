Apply to be a Math Education Revolutionary!

Do you want a join a math education startup in Vancouver aiming to help 5000 kids enjoy math 5 years above grade-level? Bravo Math is that startup.


Mission of the Math Education Revolutionary

Bravo Math is building a course called "Counting to Calculus", which will be a sequence of about 60 lessons and resources designed to convert mathphobes into calculus-lovers and graduates into amazing peer tutors. You will help build those resources so that the lessons are “fun hard” for students and minimal work for teachers.


Initial Job Duties & Workflow

  1. You will receive drafts and outlines of math education resources and asked to create digital versions and answer keys.
  2. You will receive particular learning outcomes and seek, test, create, and collect activities to achieve them.
  3. You will watch learners use your activities and iterate the resources accordingly.
  4. In the last 5 or so minutes of each work session, you will report your hours and productivity through a Google Form.
  5. About once/week, we will meet 1-on-1 to discuss your work.
  6. Propose new ways to contribute to Bravo Math.
  7. Create videos and publish videos, some for the next tutor and some for the next student.
Bravo Math is a small and new company. Job duties will change to meet the needs of students. I'll be favoring applicants who can grow with the business.


Applicant Requirements

  • Legally entitled to work in Canada.
  • Will pass a Vancouver Police Department Information Check
  • Desire to work with kids and/or the education field.
  • Excited for the uncertainty of a new business, excited to build something from nothing. I cannot, for example, tell you with certainty when or where or with whom you will work. Your job duties could change dramatically.
  • Outgoing and enthusiastic personality, the kind that kids gravitate towards, that can celebrate their progress.
  • Willingness to learn and self-examine. Ability to speak painful but important truths tactfully.
  • Can work autonomously and follow instructions. Determination and resourcefulness.
  • Culture and value compatibility. [See Bravo Math Culture Deck.]
  • If you want to work with students in-person, you must be able to get around the Vancouver area.
  • If you want to be trained as a tutor, you must sign a waiver agreeing to have your interactions with students video-recorded.


NOT Applicant Requirements

  • Significant math skill. As long as you can count fluently and accurately within 100, you can still join this company as an activity-tester. I will value your ability to empathize with struggling learners!
  • Work experience of any type.
  • Good grades in school.
  • Impressive references.
  • Any particular age, though you must be 19 or over to be in charge of kids.


Compensation

CA$18/hour to start. As you become better at this job, I expect you to propose taking on more responsibility for more pay.


Your Timeline

I am not a fan of typical job interviews.

This is what will happen instead.

  1. Read the Bravo Math Blog. Decide if you want to learn more and get involved. Feel free to email me (andrew@bravomath.com) about this and I’ll be happy to chat. If you want to work here, then...
  2. Complete an application (link below). All thoughtful applicants will receive an email response and a token $25 eTransfer payment whether or not a job offer results.
  3. If your application signals a good fit, we'll meet in-person to negotiate a temporary contract.
  4. When that's over, we'll decide on a longer-term deal.
