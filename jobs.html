Math Education Revolutionary Job Profile

Company Mission

Help 5000 kids enjoy math 5 years above grade-level. Detonate an earthquake in the stagnant world of education.

Company Strategy

Build a sequence of about 60 lessons and materials constituting “Counting to Calculus” and refine it until it’s the best damn math course ever. That means every lesson has to be (1) “fun hard” for students and (2) its resources are “open-and-go” for teachers. To accomplish this the company will:

  1. Focus on bottleneck content only, i.e. grades 1-7 number sense, then narrow slices of each of fractions, algebra, and calculus.
  2. Rapidly prototype and user-test everything. Video record students as they learn to see what works and what does not for both the learner and teacher.
  3. Seek evidence that the student hasn't learned deeply, will not retain the information, has worsened work habits, and did not enjoy the lesson. We'll be our own toughest critics.
  4. Employ the best tactics of cognitive science: active recall, spaced practice, interleaving, elaboration, multiple representations, and concrete examples.

Company Status

Bravo Math is a fairly new company with two employees. It is transitioning from 1-on-1 tutoring to a plan for global domination.

Job Mission

You will collect, build, test, and iterate resources for “Counting to Calculus” such that they create “fun hard” lessons for students and minimal work for teachers.

Job Requirements NOT Required
  • Legally entitled to work in Canada.
  • Will pass a Vancouver Police Department Information Check
  • Desire to work with kids and/or the education field.
  • Excited for the uncertainty of a new business, excited to build something from nothing. I cannot, for example, tell you with certainty when or where or with whom you will work. Your job duties could change dramatically.
  • Outgoing and enthusiastic personality, the kind that kids gravitate towards, that can celebrate their progress.
  • Willingness to learn and self-examine. Ability to speak painful but important truths tactfully.
  • Can work autonomously and follow instructions. Determination and resourcefulness.
  • Culture and value compatibility. [See Bravo Math Culture Deck.]
  • If you want to work with students in-person, you must be able to get around the Vancouver area.
  • If you want to be trained as a tutor, you must sign a waiver agreeing to have your interactions with students video-recorded.
  • Significant math skill. As long as you can count fluently and accurately within 100, you can still join this company as an activity-tester. I will value your ability to empathize with struggling learners!
  • Work experience of any type.
  • Good grades in school.
  • Impressive references.
  • Any particular age, though you must be 19 or over to be in charge of kids.

Initial Job Duties & Workflow

  1. You will receive drafts and outlines of materials by email and asked to create digital versions and answer keys.
  2. You will receive particular learning outcomes and seek, test, create, and collect activities to achieve them.
  3. You will watch learners use your activities and iterate the resources accordingly.
  4. In the last 5 or so minutes of each work session, you will report your hours and productivity through a Google Form.
  5. About once/week, we will meet 1-on-1 to discuss your work.
  6. Propose new ways to contribute to Bravo Math.
  7. Create videos and publish videos, some for the next tutor and some for the next student.
Bravo Math is a small and new company. Job duties could change drastically as the mission requires and as you prove you're capable of handling more responsibility. As mentioned above, I expect *you* to propose how this can be done (though I will have ideas as well).

Compensation

CA$18/hour to start. As you become better at this job, opportunities for more responsibility and/or raises will arise.
