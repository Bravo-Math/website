Apply to be the IT Manager (part time)

Position Summary

Are you interested in using your computer skills in a small education company trying to change the world? If so, Bravo Math is looking for someone to prove they can manage basic website (bravomath.com) needs and email functions, then, if that goes well, help the business expand with learning management systems, communication systems, social media, etc.

Job Responsibilities & Qualifications

Initial job duties will be to fix and streamline my website and email settings so that they “just work”. You will have to:

  • Manage the website. You should know how to make small HTML, JavaScript, and CSS changes to the website bravomath.com.
  • Manage email services, including “backend” things like MX records and cPanel, creating new accounts, getting it to talk to my gmail inbox, etc.
  • Prevent and respond to these kinds of errors.
  • Establish basic protocols for IT security and backups.
  • Help write part of the Bravo Math Operations Manual for IT. [This will allow you to systematize your work and qualify for more responsibilities and pay down the road.]
  • Work without supervision while tracking your hours and results.

Once you’ve done that, you should be craving more responsibilities (and compensation) and we can talk about education technology, TeX, learning management systems, communication systems, video recording, user testing labs, etc. If you have a real passion for education, that will be a huge plus.

Also required: To show you have growth potential in the company, you must be able to work in the Vancouver area.

Bonus: Students First is the first and most important core value of Bravo Math. If you prove you are currently a part-time or full-time student, your application will be favored.

Hours & Compensation

Hours: For the initial duties above, you can work whichever hours you want as long as the company's IT systems "just work". :-)

Compensation: Commensurate with your experience and competitive with industry norms. We will negotiate something that works for both of us.

Profile of Bravo Math

Bravo Math Inc. is a math education corporation located in Vancouver, Canada. Its owner and current solopreneur, Andrew Uyeno, is transitioning from 1-on-1 tutoring to building a revolutionary math education company.

Bravo Math’s first mission is to get 5000 diverse kids in the Vancouver area enjoying math 5 years above grade-level.

This would be an earthquake in a stagnant field.

Mission of the Math Education Revolutionary

Bravo Math is building the best damn math course ever called "Counting to Calculus". It will be a collection of about 60 lessons and amazing resources designed to convert mathphobes into calculus-lovers and people like YOU into amazing tutors. You will help build “fun hard” lessons for students and open-and-go resources for teachers.


Initial Job Duties & Workflow

  1. You will receive drafts and outlines of math education resources and asked to create digital versions and answer keys.
  2. You will receive particular learning outcomes and seek, test, create, and collect activities to achieve them.
  3. You will watch learners use your activities and iterate accordingly.
  4. In the last 5 or so minutes of each work session, you will report your hours and productivity through a Google Form.
  5. About once/week, we will meet 1-on-1 to discuss your work.
  6. Create videos and publish videos, some for the next tutor and some for the next student.
  7. Propose and implement new ways to contribute to the mission. Bravo Math is a small and new company, so applicants who show potential to grow with the business will be favored.


Applicant Requirements

  • Legally entitled to work in Canada.
  • Will pass a Vancouver Police Department Information Check.
  • Desire to work with kids and/or in education.
  • Excited for the uncertainty of a new business, the building of something from nothing.
  • An enthusiastic personality that inspires learners.
  • Willingness to learn and self-examine. Ability to speak painful but important truths tactfully.
  • Can work autonomously and follow instructions. Determination and resourcefulness.
  • Culture and Values compatibility.
  • If you want to work with students in-person, you must be able to get around the Vancouver area.
  • If you want to be trained as a tutor, you must sign a waiver agreeing to have your interactions with students video-recorded.


NOT Applicant Requirements

  • Significant math skill. As long as you can count fluently and accurately within 100, you can still join this company as an activity-tester. I will value your ability to empathize with struggling learners!
  • Work experience of any type.
  • Good grades in school.
  • Impressive references.
  • Any particular age, though you must be 19 or over to be legally in charge of kids.


Compensation

The default will be CA$18/hour to start unless you are exceptionally well-qualified. As you become better at the job, you will propose taking on more responsibility, performing at a higher level, and earning more. Those earnings will scale with how well you advance the mission and will thus be uncapped.
